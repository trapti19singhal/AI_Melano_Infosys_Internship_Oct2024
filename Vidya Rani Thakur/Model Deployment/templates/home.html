Welcome to MelanoAI

MelanoAI assists in early skin cancer detection by analyzing skin lesions and classifying them as benign or malignant. Our tool uses advanced AI algorithms to provide accurate and reliable results, helping in timely diagnosis and treatment decisions.

What is MelanoAI?

MelanoAI is an AI-powered tool designed to assist in the early detection of skin cancer by analyzing skin lesions. The system classifies lesions as benign or malignant with high accuracy.

How It Works

MelanoAI analyzes the uploaded image of a skin lesion using a sophisticated AI model, which determines whether the lesion is benign or malignant.

Why Use MelanoAI?

By providing fast and accurate diagnosis, MelanoAI helps in early detection of skin cancer, enabling timely interventions and better treatment outcomes.

How It Works: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Upload a clear image of a skin lesion.

Step 2: Our AI algorithm analyzes the image.

Step 3: The system classifies the lesion as benign or malignant.
