Objective

This project utilizes a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to predict the presence of skin cancer from dermatological images. The CNN model is trained to classify skin lesions as either benign or malignant based on the features extracted from the images. By leveraging deep learning techniques, the project aims to assist healthcare professionals in diagnosing skin cancer in its early stages, thereby enabling quicker and more efficient medical decisions.
